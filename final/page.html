Welcome to CSE Faculty




Dean message

Prof. Md. Mehedi Islam		 Welcome to the faculty of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at the University of Hajee Mohammad Danesh Science and Technology University, Dinajpur, Bangladesh. CSE faculty has been establish in 2004 with three offered degrees, B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering (2004), B.Sc. in Electronics and Communication Engineering (2006), and B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (2013).As Dean of the Faculty, I am proud to say that a large part of the success of our program is due to the dedication of each faculty member, a skilled student organization and highly loyal alumni. Our faculty is unique in that many members have been awarded his / her PhD degree by the reputed university abroad. It forms the basis for all faculty members who are dedicated researchers in various ways as well as teachers. In addition to all courses taught in English, faculty members' experience studying abroad contributes to international understanding as well as understanding what it is like to be a student in a foreign country. But most importantly, all members of the faculty are skilled educators, as they are actively involved in research, as witnessed by the publications in the high-ranking academic journals.
